BRAND

Our motto: All for the customer. From the genuine desire of our customers to enjoy dishes in a comfortable, elegant, luxurious, and cozy space, and to experience authentic Thai specialties at the most reasonable prices.

The clear expression of Thai cuisine is reflected in the unique presentation of the dishes and the flavors of each one. To date, we have successfully developed 350 restaurants across 10 countries in 8 years.

SPACE

The space at Thai Express combines both traditional and modern elements, while still providing diners with a strong sense of the Land of the Golden Pagoda. From the intricate decorative patterns on the walls and ceiling to the elegant design of the bar and kitchen, Thai Express truly feels like a miniature Thailand right in Vietnam.

The walls, as well as the tables and chairs, are decorated in a dominant orange-yellow tone, creating a connection with nature, just like in Thai culture.
